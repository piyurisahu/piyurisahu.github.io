‚Ä¢ Same Day Payment: Specific validation for same-day payments

2

CommonHelper Object Creation
Moving into the service layer, we set up a CommonHelper object which will carry key pieces of payment information throughout the process.
‚¨áÔ∏è
3

If the user is reusing a payee, we will validate that payee's existence from the database
‚¨áÔ∏è
4
Payer Eligibility Flow
4.1 CommonHelper Setup
A new common helper object is set up for similar reasons as above
4.2 Account Balance Check
Invoke COS or MPC to get account balance for given payer details. Right now, we are only utilizing COS, but MPC will be used in the near future.
4.3 Payment Cut-off Time
BRE is called to validate the cut off time for payment cancellation
4.4 Account Limits
AEA is called to retrieve account limits
4.5 Account Eligibility Check
BRE is called again to check payer account eligibility

5
Duplicate Payment Check
We scan both the immediate payment table (where same day business hour payments are stored) and standing order tables (where recurring payments or same day but after hours payments are stored) to find if the given payment is a duplicate based off of the payer account (cdmPartyIdentifier), the effective date of the payment, the amount, as well as the payee's account and routing number. I am currently in the process of ensuring that only active records are factored in, and that rejected or cancelled payments will not be flagged as duplicate.
‚¨áÔ∏è
6
Customer Classification
Customer's classification (regular consumer, business, or wealth) is also packaged into the common helper object based on what was returned in the payer eligibility call
‚¨áÔ∏è
7
Fraud Assessment
7.1 Data Population
Much of fraud assessment's data is populated by the supplementaryData field in the initial request
7.2 Fraud Check
All necessary data is passed to EFG for the fraud check
‚¨áÔ∏è
8
Business Hours Payment Processing
‚è∞ IF payment is occurring within business hours:
It is stored in the immediate payment table along with payer and payee information if it's not already there.
8.1 PME Notification
Payment is sent to PME
8.2 DSP Kafka Topic
Payment information is sent to DSP Kafka's INGESTED.PaymentOrder.PPO topic
8.3 Account Limits Update
Updated account limits are posted to AEA
‚¨áÔ∏è
9
After Hours/Future Dated Payment Processing
üåô IF payment is after business hours or future dated:
It will be stored instead in the standing order table where a scheduler will push it through for processing and to PME at 3:01 AM on its effective date.
9.1 Kafka Topics
Payment information is sent to DSP Kafka's INGESTED.PaymentInstruction.PPO topic, as well as the INGESTED.ServicingOrder.PPO topic
9.2 Limits Update
Updated limits are posted to AEA

Additional Notes

‚ùå PAYMENT CANCELLATION PROCESSES

üåç INTERNATIONAL CANCELLATION

1
PPO Validation
PPO validation happens. We check for valid cancellation reasons, and make sure the attempted cancellation is not a small business - small business cancellations are prohibited for international.
‚¨áÔ∏è
2
Payment Database Retrieval
In the service layer, we pull the immediate payment from the database. Only payments that are in a pending state can be cancelled, and we also need to make sure the mdmPartyId of the payment matches the one being sent in the cancellation request - this represents the user.
‚¨áÔ∏è
3
FX Quote Information Retrieval
If the transfer was cross currency and has an entry in the immediatePaymentFxQuote table, that information will be pulled as well so a cancel trade call can eventually be made.
‚¨áÔ∏è
4
CommonHelper Setup
A CommonHelperDto object is set up to carry key pieces of information throughout the process.
‚¨áÔ∏è
5
Cancellation Timeframe Validation
A call is made to BRE to check if the payment is still within the cancellation timeframe (40 minutes). If it's outside this window, the payment cannot be cancelled.
‚¨áÔ∏è
6
Fraud Assessment
If the payment is eligible for cancellation, another fraud assessment call will be made to EFG.
‚¨áÔ∏è
7
Payment Status Update
The immediate payment record will then be updated in the database to reflect the cancellation status.
‚¨áÔ∏è
8
FX Cancel Trade Call
üí± IF immediatePaymentFxQuote record exists:
Then a cancel trade call will be made to PME.
‚¨áÔ∏è
9
Customer Notification
Updated payment status and information will be sent to DSP to be viewed by the customer.
‚¨áÔ∏è
10
Account Limit Reversion
AEA limit changes made during the initial payment will be reverted.
‚¨áÔ∏è
11
Response Model Creation
Response model is created to return to the channels.

üè† DOMESTIC CANCELLATION

1
Cancellation Eligibility
Only future-dated or after hours transfers can be cancelled - same day payments made during business hours are not eligible for cancellation.
‚¨áÔ∏è
2
PPO Validation
Cancellation reason undergoes similar PPO validation as international. Other than that, the only validation that happens within the validator class is to ensure that required fields are present.
‚¨áÔ∏è
3
Standing Order Retrieval
The standing order will be pulled from the database - we make sure it's in a pending state, and has not exceeded its cancellation time frame, marked by a cancelUntilDt field in the database. As with international, we also make sure the mdmPartyId's of the original and the cancellation request match.
‚¨áÔ∏è
4
Supplementary Data Retrieval
supplementaryData is pulled from the standing order record in order to set up for another fraud assessment.
‚¨áÔ∏è
5
Fraud Assessment
Fraud assessment is sent to EFG.
‚¨áÔ∏è
6
CommonHelper Setup
CommonHelperDto object is set up to carry key pieces of information where needed.
‚¨áÔ∏è
7
Standing Order Update
Standing order record is updated in the database to reflect the cancellation.
‚¨áÔ∏è
8
Limit Changes Reversion
A call is made to AEA through our job processor to revert limit changes made from the original payment.
‚¨áÔ∏è
9
DSP Topic Updates
Updates are sent to DSP's INGESTED.PaymentOrder.PPO and INGESTED.ServicingOrder.PPO topic.
‚¨áÔ∏è
10
Retry Handling
‚ö†Ô∏è IF the calls fail:
The DSP job processor will handle them for retry later.
